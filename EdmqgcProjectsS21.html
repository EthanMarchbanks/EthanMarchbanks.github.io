Mars Explorer

In this project, I created a website to demonstrate my knowledge of AJAX. The user can provide a sol (martian day) and a list of photos taken on the given sol by the Opportunity rover will be displayed. The user can also input a date and the NASA Astronomy Photo of the Day for the given day will be displayed. All photos are fetched by AJAX requests.
